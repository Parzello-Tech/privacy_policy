Privacy Policy for Ticzy

Effective Date: 19 May 2025

Ticzy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our mobile application Ticzy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

At this time, Ticzy does not collect any personal information from users. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • We do not use or request access to device location, contacts, camera, or microphone.
  • We do not use third-party services such as Google Sign-In or Firebase.

2. Local Data Storage

All data you create within Ticzy (such as tasks, notes, financial records, etc.) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not uploaded or shared with us or any third party.

3. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future if we add new features that involve data collection or third-party services. We will notify you of any changes by updating the policy within the app and/or on our official website.
